An App for the same client as AVD. It was basically an dictionary app where user can search the words in English and can search words in URDU it has multiple features like OCR translation from to any language get the word information in English or Urdu and the English and Urdu translation is offline. The offline data is from Wikitionary.com under their public license and it is highly compressed to decrease apk size included almost 3 lac words and their description and translations.
