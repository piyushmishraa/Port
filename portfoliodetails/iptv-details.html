IPTV For Android Tv

Project information

  • Category: Android Tv
  • Client: Local Business, Germany
  • Project date: 02 March, 2019

A product for my client in Germany for his local business. It consists of two components Android TV app and a backend with one webpage. The app works with m3u and EPG files and convert the m3u files into movies series (groups, series, seasons, episodes) and Live channels with EPG and user can watch these streams within app using player.

The webpage only serves one purpose here user can upload EPG , M3U list with its mac-Address which will be shown in the app and a dashboard for my client to edit his customers info. The business model was based on subscription.

  • Stream Live channels.
  • Built in Player with custom volume and navigation controls.
  • Multilinguals (German, Turkish, English)
  • Night Shifts
  • Stream Movies and Series.
  • Favorites
  • Crash Analytics
  • Custom update deployment
  • EPG
